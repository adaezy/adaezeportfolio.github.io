RESEARCH

My research is in the intersection of machine learning and social network analysis. My work is on:

1.  Application of  graph theory and machine learning to uncover hidden connections between malicious users and groups in social networks.    

2. Application of graph theory and stochastic models to design efficient algorithms for understanding the role of information diffusion for disaster resilience in community networks affected by disaster.                                               

3. Designing algorithms for modeling resilient interdependent smart city infrastructures.
